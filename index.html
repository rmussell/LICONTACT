Privacy Policy for LinkedIn Grabber Chrome Extension

Effective Date: 10/15/2024

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the LinkedIn Grabber Chrome Extension (the "Extension").

Information We Collect

Personal Information

The Extension may collect personal information such as your LinkedIn profile data, including name, title, company, location, and email address when you use the Extension to extract profiles.

Usage Data

We collect data about your interactions with the Extension, such as the number of leads pushed and usage patterns, to improve the Extension's functionality.

Location Information

With your consent, the Extension may collect and use your geolocation data to provide location-based features.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to deliver the services and features offered by the Extension. We also use aggregated data for analytics purposes to understand how the Extension is used and to improve its functionality.

How We Share Your Information

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the Extension. We may disclose information as required by law or to comply with legal processes.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal information collected by the Extension. Please contact us for assistance.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website.
